PPP	- Price Parity, the value of 100$ in the state
GDP	- the Gross Domestic Product of the state
Average Salary - the average salary for those in the state
Average Disposable Income - the average amount of disposable income (after taxes)
Violent Crimes Committed - the amount of violent crimes each year (homicide/nonnegligent manslaughter, rape, aggravated assault, robbery)
Unemployment Rate - the unemployment rate for the state in that year (as a percent)
Hospital Count - the amount of recognized hospitals the state had in that year
traffic fatalities -  per 100k people
Air quality index - the air quality index for the state that year (on average based on the average for each county that reported the data)
